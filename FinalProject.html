Final Project


Hi everyone! This is my final project for CSC 106. Flappy Bit is a game based on the original: Flappy Bird. Flappy Bit is an interactive game that has you use the space bar as the "jump" key and fly the character through the tubes to score points. When first starting the game, the character will be seen and the tubes will start flowing from right to left. See what your high score is! Good luck and have fun!
